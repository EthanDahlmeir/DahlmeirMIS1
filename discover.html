Discover UMD

The University of Minnesota Duluth combines strong academics with an outstanding hockey program. UMD is known for its supportive community, hands-on learning opportunities, and competitive Division I athletics.

Why UMD Hockey?

UMD's hockey program has a tradition of excellence, strong coaching staff, and dedicated facilities. As a goaltender at UMD, players receive high-level training, game exposure, and a culture that prioritizes development both on and off the ice.

Campus Life

Students at UMD enjoy outdoor activities, local lakes, and a tight-knit campus community. There are many student organizations, internships with regional companies, and networking events that prepare students for careers after graduation.
